My Favourite Food is 'PIZZA'

Ingredients:

  • 2 Readymade Pizza Base
  • 1/2 cup Capsicum / BellPepper
  • 1 small Onion
  • 1 Firm Ripe Tomato
  • 1 tblsp Olives
  • 1 – 11/2 cup Amul Mozarella Cheese Grated
  • Salt to taste
  • Pepper Powder to taste
  • 1 tsp Oregano
  • 1 tsp Red Chilli Flakes
  • 1 tblsp Butter

How to make pizza with readymade pizza base:

  1. Wash and chop the capsicum and bellpeppers in long thin stripes. Also wash and chop the tomatoes only the outer skin. Remove the pulp. Similarly cube the onion in big chunks and separate the layers.
  2. To make the sauce:
  3. Take a pan, add butter, add onion and garlic until the raw smell goes away.
  4. Then tomato sauce, paste and all other ingredients and bring to boil. Turn off gas once it starts to thicken. Let it Cool.
  5. If you prefer chunky sauce use it as it is. But if you prefer silky smooth sauce then blend in a blender and then seive the mixture. Throw away the peels and skins.
  6. Keep the sauce aside. You can store this upto a week in refrigerator.
  7. To assemble the pizza
  8. Preheat the oven to 180 C.
  9. Take 2 Pizza, spread butter over the base especially on the edges.
  10. Now spread good amount of earlier prepared pizza sauce.
  11. Then sprinkle half amount of grated cheese over the sauce layer. Now place the chopped capsicum, bellpeppers, tomatoes and onions as much as you like on the cheese layer.
  12. Spread all the remaining grated cheese on top. Now place roundles of olives and if you have then fresh basil leaves. Sprinkle some oregano and red chilly flakes.
  13. Bake in a preheated oven for 10 – 11 mins on 180 C and 2-3 mins on 220 C. Take it out rest it for 2 – 3 minutes and the serve the homemade pizza.
